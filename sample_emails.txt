DATASET: PHISHING EMAILS (7 samples)
========================================

[PHISHING-01]
SENDER: no-reply@medicarе-update.xyz
SUBJECT: URGENT: Patient portal access will be SUSPENDED
BODY: Your patient portal account requires immediate verification. Click here to confirm your identity: http://bit.ly/verify-now. Failure to act will result in account closure.
ATTACHMENT: No
SIGNALS: Healthcare lure (patient portal), phishing keyword (confirm your identity), bad sender TLD (.xyz), URL shortener, urgency (all-caps)
NOTES: Classic healthcare portal phishing. Display name may spoof clinic name.

[PHISHING-02]
SENDER: support@insurance-verify.click
SUBJECT: Insurance Verification Required
BODY: Please update your billing information to avoid claim denial. Validate your credentials at http://192.168.1.1/login
ATTACHMENT: Yes
SIGNALS: Healthcare lures (insurance verification, claim denied), phishing keywords (update your billing, validate your credentials), bad TLD (.click), IP address URL
NOTES: Credential harvesting + malware attachment vector.

[PHISHING-03]
SENDER: noreply@123456789@mail.com
SUBJECT: Security Alert: Suspicious activity on your account
BODY: We detected unauthorized access to your account. Immediate response required. Reset your password now.
ATTACHMENT: No
SIGNALS: 4 phishing keywords, malformed sender (numeric username)
NOTES: Fake IT security alert. Sender address is clearly malformed.

[PHISHING-04]
SENDER: billing@healthcare-updates.net
DISPLAY NAME: Clinic Billing Department
SUBJECT: Medicare Update: Action Required
BODY: Your medicare update requires verification. Please confirm your email address at http://secure-update.healthcare-updates.net
ATTACHMENT: No
SIGNALS: Display-name spoofing, healthcare lure (medicare update), phishing keyword (confirm your email address), suspicious URL pattern
NOTES: Display-name spoof — would appear as "Clinic Billing Department" in most email clients.

[PHISHING-05]
SENDER: no-reply@account-verify.click
SUBJECT: V3RIFY YOUR ACC0UNT NOW
BODY: We have detected susp3cted login attempt on your acc0unt. Cl1ck here to v3rify your identity immediately or your acc0unt will be cl0sed.
ATTACHMENT: No
SIGNALS: Obfuscated keywords (v3rify, acc0unt, cl1ck, susp3cted), bad TLD (.click)
NOTES: Adversarial evasion attempt using character substitution. Caught by v2 obfuscation detection.

[PHISHING-06]
SENDER: support@login-yourclinic.secureportal.xyz
SUBJECT: Action Required: Confirm your login within 24 hours
BODY: Your account has been compromised. Action required within 24 hours. Failure to respond will result in account suspension. Click: http://login-yourclinic.secureportal.xyz/verify-account
ATTACHMENT: No
SIGNALS: 3 new v2 phishing keywords, bad TLD, login-URL pattern, verify-account URL pattern
NOTES: Multi-signal sophisticated attack. Uses urgency, credential threats, and URL patterns simultaneously.

[PHISHING-07]
SENDER: claims@insurance-portal.ml
DISPLAY NAME: HealthFirst Insurance Claims
SUBJECT: EOB Statement: Claim Denied — Immediate Response Required
BODY: Your recent claim has been denied. Please review your EOB statement and validate your credentials to file an appeal. Attach your prior authorization and submit to http://bit.ly/claims-appeal
ATTACHMENT: Yes
SIGNALS: Display-name spoof, EOB lure, claim denied lure, phishing keywords, bad TLD (.ml), URL shortener, attachment
NOTES: Highest-complexity attack in dataset. Triggers 8 separate flags.
